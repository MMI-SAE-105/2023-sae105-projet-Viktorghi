About me

My name is Viktor Ghirardelli, and I'm a first-year MMI student in Montbéliard. I've chosen to explore the theme of the "replica" using the film "Chihiro's Journey" and the following quote: "You never forget the people you've met, you just have trouble remembering them" by Zeniba.

My project, called "Spirits", aims to help people discover or rediscover the work and world of the film "The Voyage of Chihiro". The emphasis is on nostalgia and the discovery of little-known elements of this universe.
